Project Spitfire


Backstory

My father bought this Spitfire a long time ago as present for my mother. They have made a lot of trips to different countries in europa. I can also remember a ride with my father when I was 7 year old. In 2010 the car unfortunately broke down and was then taken to a car storage facility. After 14 years in 2024 my father told me", the only sun doing a technical study, "do you want to repaire the Spitfire otherwise am I going to cell the car". And that was the moment the journy stated.
Below are the milestones of the process.
